Description

Money Manager App is an application developed to contain financial accounts and transactions and, ultimately, show your financial status.
Note: the app is in development stage and the version 1.0 is not complete.

Important note

When downloading the app, you could get a warning related to the untrusted nature of the current application. If you trust the authors (us), you can choose to open the application despite the warnings.
